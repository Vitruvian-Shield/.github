# Vitruvian Shield

Vitruvian Shield builds the infrastructure that makes decentralised and hybrid clinical trials work. One AI-native platform combines clinical trial management, electronic data capture, electronic consent, electronic patient-reported outcomes, remote patient monitoring and regulatory output generation, engineered as Software as a Medical Device under EU MDR and fit for 21 CFR Part 11 use.

## What we do

We operate at the intersection of regulated clinical research and consumer-grade digital experience. Our platform is used by sponsors, contract research organisations, academic medical centres and integrated care networks to run clinical studies and remote patient monitoring programmes across Europe and, progressively, beyond.

## Where we are

Five group entities across Switzerland, Portugal, Armenia and the United Arab Emirates. The holding is domiciled in Switzerland.

## Current programmes

- ULS São João oncology pilot, Porto, Portugal
- PT-EPI-DCT-002 epilepsy decentralised study, Portugal
- Integrated care pathway pilot with SMN (VIVA pathway)
- Clinical engagement with Centre Hospitalier Universitaire Vaudois

## Engineering principles

We build for regulated environments first. Every service we publish is treated as potentially safety-impacting until proven otherwise by a documented IEC 62304 safety classification. Every change is reviewed against 21 CFR Part 11 audit trail and electronic signature requirements. Every dependency is scanned. Every release carries a CycloneDX software bill of materials. Every commit is signed.
